Delete Your Account

Bhoomi by Bhoomi Creatives

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your Bhoomi account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to our support address with the subject line "Delete my account". Please use the email address associated with your Bhoomi account so we can verify and process your request.

Email Us to Delete Account

We will process your request within 7 days and send a confirmation once complete.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile and email address
  • Your saved favourites and watch history
  • Your family/ancestor records stored in the app
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

What Is Not Affected

  • Anonymous usage statistics (not linked to your identity)
  • Content you have not personally submitted

Guest Users

If you used the app as a guest (without signing in), no personal data was stored on our servers. Simply uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Questions?
